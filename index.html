About Me.


A passionate student working at the intersection of computer science and scientific research to share my ideas with the world. A recent high school graduate from Nusrat Senior Secondary School with BIG tech wins. I am currently serving as a Data Science Intern at the Medical Research Council Unit The Gambia at the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine. Formerly, I served as the Lead AI and Machine Learning Intern at Obentas Global Technology Company.
From a young age, I've always been interested in technology and how it can be used to make the world a better place, and asking existential questions about how computational methods can solve real-world challenges. This passion led me to start learning about web development and machine learning, and I soon realized that I could use my skills to create meaningful projects that would make a difference.
While in high school, I have started initiatives based on research and tech. I published a scientific research work title Lowel AI Using Machine Learning Algorithms and BLA Sensors to detect harmful bacteria present in water, an AI device that aims to detect harmful bacteria present in water. I envision launching the StemBia initiative which aims to use research and engineering solutions to solve daily problems faced by Gambians. At Nusrat Senior Secondary, I served as the president and Lead Instructor of the ICT club for academic year 2023/2024. AI is my obsession.
My youtube channel CBJtech, serves as a platform for sharing my insights on programming, AI and tech in general.
When not coding, working on a research or working on tech related, I enjoy playing sport, spending time with family and eating too much chocolate. I currently reside in The Gambia.
