About Me

Quick Facts

  • 🎓 Computer Science freshman @ University of The Gambia
  • 🔬 Prev. Data Science Intern @ MRC Unit The Gambia
  • 🎤 Tech Speaker & Community Educator
  • 📹 YouTuber @ CBJtech
  • 🌍 Based in Yundum, The Gambia

I’m a first year Computer Science student and AI practitioner focused on building practical, deployable AI systems for healthcare, education, and community impact in The Gambia and across Africa.

My journey started at Nusrat Senior Secondary School, where curiosity about technology grew into running one of the most active ICT Clubs in the country, publishing research, and mentoring younger students. After graduating highshool, I worked as a Data Science Intern at the Medical Research Council Unit The Gambia, part of the London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine

Today, I work on computer vision and deep learning projects, including pneumonia detection, medical imaging, and tools that help local teams access data and research more easily.

Through talks, workshops, and my YouTube channel CBJtech, I share the lessons, mistakes, and wins from my journey so more young Africans can see themselves in AI and scientific research.
